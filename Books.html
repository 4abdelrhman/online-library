Little Women

Louisa May Alcott

Little Women follows the four March sisters—Jo, Beth, Meg, and Amy—as they navigate life, love, and personal growth in Civil War-era New England. Inspired by Louisa May Alcott's own family, the novel explores themes of sisterhood, ambition, and resilience. Though written as a "girl's book," its timeless messages have made it a classic for all readers.
